First Alert Weather
Action News Jax

A live broadcast bumper for Action News Jax's First Alert Weather franchise, built to open every weather segment with the same dimensional, glass-and-steel studio look.

The set was modeled and rendered in Cinema 4D to give the graphic real depth and reflection, then composited and finished in After Effects for a clean handoff into the live broadcast.

The bumper anchors a full on-air campaign, with the same look extended into a motion graphics package, digital ads, and social assets.
