Win Tickets Form
Giveaway Entry · Confirmation State

A giveaway entry form built around one job: get someone from "tell me more" to "I'm entered" with as little friction as possible. Name, email, an optional phone number, a ticket-count stepper, and a short answer field lead into a single gradient CTA.

Submitting the form transitions into a confirmation state — a checkmark, a "you're entered" pill, and share buttons for spreading the word — so the entry feels complete rather than just submitted into a void.

Designed and built with Claude and Figma AI, from the first layout pass through the gradient-and-pill visual language carried across both states.
