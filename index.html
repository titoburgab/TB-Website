About me

Hi, I'm Tito.
I craft bold visuals
for stories that move.

I'm an experienced designer and new web developer with a hands-on journey that's taken me around the world. I've been a fisherman in Peru, a mechanic in Venezuela, a welder in Japan, a production designer in Spain, and a creative services and art director in the U.S.

All of that feeds into how I build today: curious, adaptable, and always focused on creating digital experiences that feel simple, human, and useful.
